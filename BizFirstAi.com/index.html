This dynamic framework represents the evolving architecture of Agentic AI — a future-facing approach where AI doesn't just predict but acts autonomously.

The AI continuously ingests data, trains itself, updates knowledge, and adapts via feedback and scanning.

  • Data Ingestion
  • Self Training
  • Knowledge Updates
  • Feedback Adaptation

Efficiency & Refinement

Optimizes resource usage, tunes performance, and ensures quality and improvement.

  • Resource Optimization
  • Performance Tuning
